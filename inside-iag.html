Inside IAG

IAG Travel & Logistics specializes in end-to-end travel management and logistical support for entertainment professionals, corporate teams, and individual leisure travelers.

With deep roots in the entertainment industry, we understand the demands of life on the road—working alongside artist management, coordinating events, and overseeing production logistics. We also recognize the complex and sensitive nature of this business, handling every detail with professionalism and discretion.

In addition, our team brings extensive knowledge of corporate and leisure travel, allowing us to deliver seamless experiences for executives, teams, and individual travelers alike.

We rely on a well-established network of connections, which allows us to tailor each travel package to perfectly suit each client’s unique needs.

“Our travel advisors function as a seamless extension of your team. Whether managing the logistics of a multi-city music tour, coordinating a high-level travel for executives or curating a memorable family vacation, we deliver strategic planning, personalized service, and exceptional execution every step of the way.”

Marta Iglesias, Founder, IAG Travel & Logistics
