Inside IAG

IAG Travel & Logistics specializes in providing travel management and logistics for the entertainment industry. We service Entertainers, Executives, Concert Tours, Film & TV Productions, Fashion, Sporting and Special Events.

We have extensive experience in the entertainment industry and know all about being on the road, working for artist management, coordinating events and overseeing production. In addition we understand the complicated and sensitive nature of the business.

We have an established network of connections throughout the entertainment and travel industries with in-the-know colleagues. We know the best in the business which allows us to customize each package for each situation.

“Our travel advisors function as a seamless extension of your team. Whether managing the logistics of a multi-city music tour, coordinating a high-level travel for executives or curating a memorable family vacation, we deliver strategic planning, personalized service, and exceptional execution every step of the way.”

Marta Díaz, IAG Travel Agency Owner.
